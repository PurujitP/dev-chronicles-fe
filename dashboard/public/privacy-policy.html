DevChronicles Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024
TL;DR: DevChronicles only tracks developer-related websites (Stack Overflow, GitHub, documentation). Your personal browsing stays private. All data is encrypted and you control it completely.

1. Information We Collect

What We Track:

  • Developer Site Visits: URLs and titles from coding-related websites only (Stack Overflow, GitHub, documentation sites, tutorial sites)
  • Visit Metadata: Timestamps, time spent, and visit frequency for research analytics
  • Account Information: Google account email and name for authentication
  • Usage Analytics: Categorized research patterns (bugs fixed, concepts learned, tools explored)

What We DON'T Track:

  • Personal websites, social media, shopping, entertainment
  • Non-technical content or general browsing
  • Private/incognito browsing sessions
  • Sensitive personal information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Learning Analytics: Generate insights about your developer research patterns
  • Progress Tracking: Show statistics on concepts learned and problems solved
  • Personalization: Provide customized research insights and recommendations
  • Synchronization: Sync your research data across devices securely

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Local Storage: Data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Sync: Optional encrypted cloud storage for cross-device access
  • Retention: Data is kept only as long as you use the service

4. Data Sharing

We Never Share Your Data. Your research patterns, browsing history, and personal information are never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.
  • No Third Parties: Data stays within DevChronicles systems only
  • No Advertising: We don't show ads or sell data to advertisers
  • No Analytics Partners: No external tracking or analytics services

5. Google Integration

DevChronicles uses Google OAuth for authentication:

  • Permissions: We request only email and profile access
  • Purpose: Authentication and account identification only
  • Data Handling: Google data is handled per Google's privacy policies

6. Chrome Extension Permissions

  • History: Required to analyze developer research patterns
  • Storage: For caching data and preferences locally
  • Identity: For Google authentication
  • Host Permissions: To sync data with DevChronicles servers

7. Children's Privacy

DevChronicles is designed for developers and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this service.

8. International Users

DevChronicles operates globally. By using our service, you consent to the processing of your data in accordance with this privacy policy, regardless of your location.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Users will be notified of significant changes through the extension interface. Continued use constitutes acceptance of updates.

10. Open Source & Transparency

DevChronicles believes in transparency. Our extension code is available for review, and we maintain clear documentation of our data practices.
